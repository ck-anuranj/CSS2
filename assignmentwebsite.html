Nature

A tree is a tall plant that can live a very long time. It has a single stem or trunk and branches that support leaves. Beneath the ground, a tree has a root system that acts as an anchor and stores the water and nutrients the plant needs to grow. One way we distinguish trees from other plants is their thick and rigid ligneous tissues, which we know as wood. Of course, different tree species have different types of wood.
